The Future
Fall 2022 The Hydrovia team will begin testing the first MVP in the Fall in preparation for a larger roll-out over the next year.
Summer 2022 Work on the first full-scale working prototype began at the start of the summer and is currently ongoing. Great strides are being made in the biotechnology and the robotics of the ALGAE system.
Fall 2021 Chase Leffers began exploring algae technology as a solution to food scarcity and sustainability in 2021. This exploration would spark the creation of Hydrovia.
